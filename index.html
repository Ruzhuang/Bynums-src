However, our modified version of Bynum's game imposes the following additional restriction:

First, a single row or column that no longer has any neighbors is said to be "isolated" (such rows or columns are no longer attached to any other rows or columns.)
Isolated rows or columns are automatically erased once they are generated by a move.
