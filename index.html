    The game goes on like this; And, eventually, the 6x8 starting position is completely erased.The game is won by the player who makes the last legal move. That is, if it happens to be your turn and nothing remains on the board, then you lose!

    However, our modified version of Bynum's game imposes the following additional restriction:

    A single row or column that is no longer attached to any other part is said to be “isolated”. Such rows or columns are automatically erased once they are generated by a move.
